Ingeniously simple operation

Simply tap to switch on or off, three speeds, sleep mode with dimmed display and ultra-quiet power level, automatic mode which matches speed to current humidity. Controlling the Origi¬nal Connect Humidifier for ideal indoor air qual¬ity couldn’t be easier. The display also indicates when actions are due, making the AH510/515 Original Connect even easier to use. Thanks to the integrated hygrostat, the display indicates and controls the air humidity in real time.

Unique humidification technology

A fan directs the ambient air into the water-fiilled humidifier, where it flows through specially arrayed disk stacks. Hygienically clean air evaporates from their surfaces into the room. Simultaneously, the disks trap harm¬ful substances such as dust or pollen, which are then washed off in the water and collected in the humidifier. This is the most efficient, hy¬gienic and natural way of humidifying air with¬out producing any scaling and condensation
